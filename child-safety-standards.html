Storytus - Child Safety Standards

Application: Storytus
Developer: Storytus Team
Last Updated: January 2025

Zero Tolerance Policy: Storytus explicitly prohibits all forms of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) content.

1. Prohibited Content

Storytus strictly prohibits:

  • Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) material
  • Any content that sexualizes, endangers, or exploits minors
  • Grooming behavior or predatory conduct toward children
  • Content that depicts, encourages, or promotes child abuse

2. Content Moderation

  • User-generated content is monitored for CSAE violations
  • Automated and manual review systems in place
  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Permanent ban of offending accounts

3. Reporting Mechanism

Users can report CSAE content through:

  • In-app reporting feature
  • Email: niyondikojoffreasjubu@gmail.com
  • All reports are reviewed within 24 hours

4. Cooperation with Authorities

Storytus cooperates fully with law enforcement and reports all CSAE content to:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Local law enforcement agencies
  • Relevant international authorities

5. Age Restrictions

  • Minimum age requirement: 13 years old
  • Parental consent required for users under 18
  • Age verification mechanisms in place

6. Privacy Protection for Minors

  • Enhanced privacy settings for users under 18
  • Restricted visibility of minor profiles
  • Limited data collection for minors
